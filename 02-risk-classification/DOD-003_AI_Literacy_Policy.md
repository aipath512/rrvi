# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish the principles and requirements for achieving and maintaining an appropriate level of Artificial Intelligence literacy within AiVenture SRL.

This policy supports compliance with Article 4 of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act), requiring providers and deployers of AI systems to ensure that personnel possess sufficient AI literacy according to their roles and responsibilities.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- all employees;
- company management;
- contractors;
- consultants;
- interns;
- temporary personnel;
- any individual using AI systems on behalf of AiVenture SRL.

---

# 3. Policy Statements

AiVenture SRL shall ensure that personnel using AI systems possess an appropriate level of AI literacy.

AI literacy shall be proportionate to:

- job responsibilities;
- technical knowledge;
- experience;
- AI systems used;
- potential impact of AI-assisted activities.

---

# 4. AI Literacy Objectives

The organization shall ensure that personnel understand:

- fundamental AI concepts;
- capabilities and limitations of AI systems;
- risks associated with AI;
- human oversight responsibilities;
- data protection obligations;
- information security principles;
- ethical use of AI;
- applicable regulatory requirements.

---

# 5. Training Requirements

AI literacy activities may include:

- awareness sessions;
- internal workshops;
- formal training;
- online learning;
- practical demonstrations;
- self-study supported by company guidance.

Training shall be documented.

---

# 6. Roles and Responsibilities

## Managing Director

Approves the AI Literacy Policy.

---

## AI Governance Owner

Responsible for:

- coordinating AI literacy activities;
- maintaining literacy records;
- reviewing competency levels.

---

## Department Managers

Responsible for:

- ensuring staff participation;
- identifying additional training needs.

---

## Employees

Responsible for:

- participating in required training;
- applying AI responsibly;
- reporting knowledge gaps when identified.

---

# 7. Review Requirements

This policy shall be reviewed whenever:

- significant AI systems are introduced;
- regulatory requirements change;
- organizational responsibilities change;
- major AI-related incidents occur.

At minimum, the policy shall be reviewed annually.

---

# 8. Compliance

Compliance with this policy is mandatory.

Failure to maintain adequate AI literacy may increase operational, legal and regulatory risks.
