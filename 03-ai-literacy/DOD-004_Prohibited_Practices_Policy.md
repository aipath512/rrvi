# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to prohibit the development, acquisition, deployment or use of Artificial Intelligence systems that are classified as prohibited under Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act).

AiVenture SRL is committed to ensuring that no prohibited AI practice is intentionally implemented or operated within the organization.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- all AI systems recorded in the AI System Inventory;
- internally developed AI systems;
- third-party AI services;
- proof-of-concept projects;
- production systems;
- contractors and consultants acting on behalf of AiVenture SRL.

---

# 3. Policy Statement

AiVenture SRL shall not develop, deploy, purchase, integrate or operate AI systems prohibited by the EU AI Act.

Every AI system shall undergo a Prohibited AI Practices assessment before operational deployment.

---

# 4. Prohibited AI Practices

The following categories shall not be implemented by AiVenture SRL unless expressly permitted by applicable law:

- harmful manipulation or deceptive techniques;
- exploitation of vulnerabilities based on age, disability or social circumstances;
- social scoring by public authorities;
- individual criminal risk prediction based solely on profiling;
- untargeted scraping of facial images for facial recognition databases;
- emotion recognition in prohibited contexts;
- biometric categorisation using sensitive characteristics where prohibited;
- prohibited real-time remote biometric identification.

---

# 5. Preventive Controls

AiVenture SRL shall:

- maintain an AI System Inventory;
- perform Risk Classification;
- perform Prohibited AI assessment;
- document assessment results;
- maintain Human Oversight;
- retain objective evidence.

---

# 6. Responsibilities

## Managing Director

Approves this policy.

---

## AI Governance Owner

Responsible for:

- coordinating prohibited practice assessments;
- maintaining documentation;
- reporting non-compliance.

---

## AI System Owners

Responsible for:

- declaring intended use;
- notifying functional changes;
- supporting assessments.

---

## Compliance Owner

Responsible for:

- monitoring regulatory developments;
- validating assessments;
- supporting inspections.

---

# 7. Review Requirements

This policy shall be reviewed:

- annually;
- after amendments to the EU AI Act;
- after significant AI system changes;
- following regulatory guidance updates.

---

# 8. Compliance

Compliance with this policy is mandatory.

Any identified prohibited AI practice shall be immediately escalated to management and removed from operational use.
