# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish the principles and requirements for creating, maintaining, reviewing and preserving Technical Documentation for Artificial Intelligence systems used, developed, deployed or integrated by AiVenture SRL.

This policy supports compliance with Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (EU AI Act) by ensuring that technical documentation is complete, accurate, traceable and available for internal governance and regulatory inspection.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- internally developed AI systems;
- externally acquired AI systems;
- AI systems under evaluation;
- AI systems integrated into products or services;
- proof-of-concept projects;
- production AI systems.

---

# 3. Policy Statement

AiVenture SRL shall maintain Technical Documentation for every AI system proportionate to:

- regulatory requirements;
- system complexity;
- intended purpose;
- operational risk;
- business impact.

Technical Documentation shall be maintained throughout the lifecycle of each AI system.

---

# 4. Documentation Principles

Technical Documentation shall be:

- complete;
- accurate;
- consistent;
- traceable;
- version controlled;
- regularly reviewed;
- available for inspection.

---

# 5. Minimum Documentation Content

Where applicable, Technical Documentation shall include:

- AI System Identification;
- Intended Purpose;
- Functional Description;
- System Architecture;
- Data Sources;
- Training Data (if applicable);
- Inputs and Outputs;
- Human Oversight;
- Risk Classification;
- High-Risk Assessment;
- Performance Characteristics;
- Limitations;
- Security Controls;
- Change History.

---

# 6. Roles and Responsibilities

## Managing Director

Approves this policy.

---

## AI Governance Owner

Responsible for:

- maintaining Technical Documentation governance;
- coordinating document reviews;
- ensuring document availability during inspections.

---

## AI System Owners

Responsible for:

- preparing Technical Documentation;
- maintaining document accuracy;
- reporting significant changes.

---

## Compliance Owner

Responsible for:

- verifying documentation completeness;
- monitoring regulatory requirements;
- supporting inspections and audits.

---

# 7. Review Requirements

Technical Documentation shall be reviewed:

- annually;
- before production deployment;
- after significant system changes;
- after regulatory updates;
- before regulatory inspections.

---

# 8. Compliance

Compliance with this policy is mandatory.

Incomplete or outdated Technical Documentation shall be corrected before continued operational use where required.
